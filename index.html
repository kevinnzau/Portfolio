workflow

1.Brief

Leave a request or call me on +254715952372. I will send you a short brief in response.

2.Interview

Oh, one of my favourite moments, I conduct an interview with prepared questions for your project.

3.Contract

Determine the total cost & terms of the project. We conclude a contract. After you have paid 50% of the advance payment, I proceed to the next stages of work.

4.Copywriting

I make the pure text structure of the site. With great texts about your product, service and brand.

5.Prototyping

I create a website prototype. Thinking over the logic, pages, process, forms etc.

6.Design

I create the design of all pages on the site. I will adapt the design for all resolutions. Create a cool design of all mobile versions.

7.Development

Develop the website from scratch or a CMS platform as agreed beforehand.

8.Testing

Verification is done on the interface functionality, browser support compatibility, and performance.

9.Deployment

Arrangements are made on the websites custom domain name, web hosting and the site is made available to the world wide web.
